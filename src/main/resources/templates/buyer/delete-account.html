Please carefully read our policies and terms before proceeding with deletion.
  • You will permanently lose all your data, points, rewards, and any other items associated with your RedHub account.
  • Ensure your account has no pending transactions, active subscriptions, or ongoing processes that require this account.
  • RedHub may retain some transaction or activity data for a limited period for auditing and legal purposes after successful deletion, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
  • After successful deletion, you will no longer be able to log in or access the deleted account.
  • The email address and phone number linked to the deleted account cannot be used to register a new RedHub account or link to another existing account for a certain period.
  • If this account is used to access any associated services (e.g., premium features, partner portals), access to those services will also be revoked.
